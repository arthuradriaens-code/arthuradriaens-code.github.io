Exam advice

If possible, look at previous exams and look for reoccuring patterns. This can be the score distribution (in Ghent university this was theory: 12 and exercises: 8 although this was also mentioned in the discription of the course), certain theoretical proofs that the professor tends to find important (and thus where you should lay your focus a bit more), or a particular kind of exercise like for example in atomic and molecular physics a lot of the times there was an exercise on spin-spin coupling (which you should thus best master).
